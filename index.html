ABOUT ME

My name is Brittany Boughter and I am new to the tech world but love the challenge. For the last ten years, I was working as a police dispatcher. Coding has reminded me of my childhood and watching my mom as a dispatcher. Using command lines are an intregal part of dispatching even today and it's exciting to learn how the gibberish gets translated by the computer. So far I have taken a prepatory class with LearningFuze and hope to continue my education with the full-time program. I have a passion for sports and am seeking a career that is tech and sports related.

My inspiration to switch careers comes from my want to spend more family time with my husband and kids. Even though I know that I need to work hard to get myself established, this career still provides me more flexibilty. I was raised on the idea of needing to put your career first to provide for your family but I have learned recently that life is to short and there needs to be a healthy balance.
